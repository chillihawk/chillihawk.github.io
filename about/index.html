About me


I am an experienced developer and technical lead working principally on the Microsoft stack, predominantly service side.

I have over 10 years experience working in financial services technology both as a sole contributor and as a manager and technical lead.

My main areas of professional interest are: efficiency (individuals, teams, practices and processes), services (I know a great deal about WCF and am a practitioner (always learning) of the iDesign method, Microsoft Excel (I've spent a lot of time wrangling trader spreadsheets and cut my programming teeth on VBA) and teaching others.

Outside of work I have a wife (Sophie) and 3 young kids (Samson, Gabriel and Meadow) and we live in Bournemouth on the South Coast of England. In my spare time I love coffee, red wine, skiing, rugby, cricket and running.
